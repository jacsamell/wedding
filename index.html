Our Story

We met while exploring the world, two souls drawn together by wanderlust and adventure. Our love story unfolded across continents, from the bustling streets of the UK to the sun-drenched shores of Australia.

After a year of traveling to every corner of our animated world, we're ready to plant our roots beneath the golden elm tree in Sarah's family garden, where we'll say "I do" surrounded by those we love most.
